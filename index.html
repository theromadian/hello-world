About Me

Born in Canada to Romanian parents, I had a pretty unique experience growing up. I started learning french in 2nd grade as a second language. I played hockey but not nearly as much as you'd think a canadian would. I moved to the U.S. when I was 9, and began experimenting with making short films with my brother in our backyard and around our house. I made a YouTube channel at the age of 13, and consistently posted gaming content and whatever short films I made in my spare time. I knew even then that I wanted to pursue a creative field, and 10 years later I'm still creating. In my free time you can find me streaming on Twitch, watching NBA basketball, or listening to records (I can never collect too many).
